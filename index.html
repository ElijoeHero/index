STORY

The Lunar Limit is an epic story of a galaxy called otmos. Otmos is a universe comprised of complete darkness as a result of its consentrated dark matter. This allows the galaxy to connect with everything that touches or intercepts with dark matter something like a spider web. This also allows the otmos to want to experience these elements in its own universe, but all hope wasn'y lost. Every millenia, the dark matter in the otmos becomes so consentrated, that the universe is able to form its own solids as a result. So this would be the moment it would create its own planets, stars and would even attempt life. When the event took place, the universe manifested planets moons stars and even life, but the outcome was not what the Otmos ever considered. The dark stars became flying meteors that would fire off at anything around it. The moons became oribiting dark holes that would deteriorate anything around it. The planets grew giant mouths and would devour other planets around it. Then there were the life forms called Murk. These beings sought straight destruction seeking to devour everything in it's path. This became the universe of the otmos. Also, Because the universe is connected to dark matter all around the universe, these beings began to attack other galaxies and planets destroying them and making the otmos powerful shrouding the cosmos with more and more darkness. This was not the intentions of the otmos, but it seemed to be it's fate. Time had passed and the darkness had taken over a great amount of planets in the universe. But there was an unexplained occurence that would change everything. All of a sudden, there was a brilliant rock huge in size that flew through the otmos.
